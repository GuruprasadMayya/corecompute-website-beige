Engineering services across the AI infrastructure lifecycle

From facility architecture to GPU procurement, deployment and long-term operations — every discipline required to stand up and sustain mission-critical AI compute.

AI Data Centre Design

Facility architecture and planning for AI-ready sites — power, cooling and spatial design engineered around the density profile of modern GPU compute, from whitespace layout through to shell-and-core coordination.

02 / 10

GPU Infrastructure

Design, sourcing and deployment of NVIDIA GPU clusters engineered for AI training and inference — covering compute topology, interconnect strategy and rack-level power and thermal planning.

03 / 10

Enterprise Memory Solutions

Supply and integration of enterprise-grade memory sized and configured for AI workload throughput, with component-level planning that accounts for bandwidth, capacity and workload-specific bottlenecks.

04 / 10

AI Networking

High-speed network fabric architecture for GPU clusters and HPC environments — including topology design, transceiver and switch selection, and latency-aware cabling strategy.

05 / 10

High Performance Storage

Low-latency storage platforms sized for enterprise AI applications — from checkpointing and training datasets to inference-serving tiers with predictable I/O performance.

06 / 10

Blueprint & Rack Layout Design

Infrastructure planning documentation — rack elevation drawings, cooling layouts and deployment blueprints engineered for constructability and coordinated with electrical and mechanical trades.

07 / 10

Large Scale Infrastructure Deployment

End-to-end implementation of enterprise AI data centre programmes — coordinating procurement, physical build-out, cluster integration and commissioning under a single delivery plan.

08 / 10

Data Centre Operations

Resident engineering teams managing mission-critical AI infrastructure day-to-day — monitoring, incident response and operational governance aligned to enterprise SLAs.

09 / 10

Data Centre Maintenance

Preventive and corrective maintenance programmes engineered to protect uptime — structured inspection cycles, spares strategy and rapid-response fault recovery.

CTO Advisory Services

Strategic technology leadership for enterprise infrastructure decisions — capacity planning, architecture guidance and vendor-neutral advisory for CIOs and CTOs.
